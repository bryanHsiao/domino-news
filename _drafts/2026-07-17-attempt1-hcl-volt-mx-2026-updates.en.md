## Overview of HCL Volt MX 2026 Updates

HCL Volt MX, a leading low-code application development platform, introduced a series of significant updates in 2026 aimed at enhancing developer productivity and application security.

### Key Updates

- **Security Enhancements**:
  - On June 22, 2026, the release of V10 Servicepack 0 Fixpack 8 Hotfix 0 implemented various security hardening measures to ensure application safety and stability. For more details, refer to the [HCL Volt MX Release Notes](https://help.hcl-software.com/voltmx/latest/VMX_release_notes.html).

- **Figma Design Import**:
  - The V10 version introduced a feature allowing developers to import designs directly from Figma into Iris, enabling seamless conversion of Figma designs into functional application interfaces, thereby reducing development time. More information is available in the blog post [HCL Volt MX v10 Darwin: From Figma to App, AIAD, CarPlay, and Beyond](https://www.hcl-software.com/blog/volt-mx/hcl-volt-mx-v10-darwin-from-figma-to-app-aiad-carplay-and-beyond).

- **AI-Augmented Development**:
  - The platform incorporated Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology, leveraging AI to enhance the development process, assisting developers in building applications more efficiently.

- **CarPlay Support**:
  - Added support for Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, enabling developers to create applications for automotive platforms, expanding the reach and utility of their applications.

### Training and Certification

To assist developers in mastering these new features, HCL conducted the HCL Volt MX Foundations Training and Certification course from April 13 to May 8, 2026. This 8-day course, with 4-hour daily sessions, covered topics such as low-code, pro-code, and integration capabilities. Details can be found at [HCL Volt MX Foundations Training and Certification (04-13-2026)](https://hclsoftwareu.hcl-software.com/calendar-view/hcl-volt-mx/hcl-volt-mx-foundations-training-and-certification-04-13-2026/721).

These updates and training initiatives underscore HCL's commitment to enhancing the Volt MX platform's capabilities and supporting developers in building robust and secure applications.
