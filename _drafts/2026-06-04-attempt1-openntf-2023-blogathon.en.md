On September 29, 2023, OpenNTF announced the launch of the 2023 Blogathon, an initiative designed to encourage community members to write and share technical articles related to HCL Domino. ([openntf.org](https://www.openntf.org/blogs/openntf.nsf?utm_source=openai))This event provides a platform for developers and enthusiasts to showcase their expertise and foster knowledge exchange and innovation within the community.

Participants are invited to submit articles covering a wide range of topics, including but not limited to:

- New features and best practices in HCL Domino
- Development tips and examples
- Case studies and success stories

OpenNTF emphasizes that this event is not only an opportunity to share knowledge but also a means to enhance personal and professional branding.Submitted articles will be published on OpenNTF's official blog, reaching a broader audience.

In addition, OpenNTF has introduced a digital badges program to recognize contributions from community members. ([openntf.org](https://www.openntf.org/blogs/openntf.nsf?utm_source=openai))These badges will be awarded to participants who excel in the Blogathon, serving as acknowledgment of their contributions.

OpenNTF encourages all developers and enthusiasts interested in HCL Domino and related technologies to participate in the Blogathon, contributing to the growth and innovation of the community.
