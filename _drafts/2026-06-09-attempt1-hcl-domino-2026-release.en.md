HCL Domino 2026 (version 14.5.1) was officially released on March 19, 2026, providing enterprises with a modernized and secure collaboration platform. Here are the key features and improvements in this release:

## New User Interface

Domino 2026 introduces cleaner layouts, modern navigation, and updated iconography, shaped by over 1,000 community votes to better align with user workflows. ([hcl-software.com](https://www.hcl-software.com/domino/home?utm_source=openai))

## Integrated AI Capabilities

HCL Domino IQ now incorporates Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), enabling teams to securely query organizational knowledge stored in Domino databases using natural language, with data remaining under internal control. ([hcl-software.com](https://www.hcl-software.com/domino/home?utm_source=openai))

## Enhanced Security

Domino 2026 has achieved BSI IT-Grundschutz certification, offering enterprise-grade security, and introduces phishing-resistant Passkey authentication to ensure the highest levels of data privacy and security. ([hcl-software.com](https://www.hcl-software.com/domino/home?utm_source=openai))

## Improved REST API and JSON Parsing

Updated REST APIs and enhanced LotusScript JSON parsing reduce the effort required to integrate Domino with modern development ecosystems and third-party tools. ([hcl-software.com](https://www.hcl-software.com/domino/home?utm_source=openai))

## Support for Latest Operating Systems

Domino 2026 now supports IBM i 7.6, Red Hat 10, and Windows Server 2025, ensuring that your infrastructure and Domino investments can advance in sync. ([hcl-software.com](https://www.hcl-software.com/domino/home?utm_source=openai))

## Download and Upgrade

Installation kits for Domino 2026 are now available through the My HCLSoftware Download Portal. Existing users are encouraged to upgrade promptly to take advantage of the new features and enhanced security. ([developer.ds.hcl-software.com](https://developer.ds.hcl-software.com/t/hcl-domino-2026-14-5-1-is-here/172452?utm_source=openai))
