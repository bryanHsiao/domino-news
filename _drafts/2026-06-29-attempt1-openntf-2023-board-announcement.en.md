OpenNTF has recently announced its board members for the 2023-2024 term. The board includes four returning member directors: Doug Robinson, Jesse Gallagher, Serdar Basegmez, and Heiko Voigt. Newly elected member directors are Dan Dumont, Paul Withers, Oliver Busse, and Graham Acres, each beginning a two-year term. Additionally, Roberto Boccadoro, Keith Brooks, and Chris Bekas have been elected as contributor directors for a one-year term. The organization expresses gratitude to Howard Greenberg for his service as he steps down from the board. The new board will convene its first meeting on October 11 to elect positions such as Chairman, Treasurer, and Secretary.
