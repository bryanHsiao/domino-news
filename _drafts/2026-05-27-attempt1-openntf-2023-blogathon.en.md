On September 29, 2023, OpenNTF announced the 2023 Blogathon, aiming to encourage community members to write and share technical articles related to HCL Domino and its ecosystem. ([openntf.org](https://www.openntf.org/blogs/openntf.nsf?utm_source=openai))

**Event Details**

- **Topics**: Participants are invited to write about subjects related to HCL Domino, HCL Notes, Domino REST API, Volt MX, HCL Nomad, AppDev Pack, Sametime, OpenNTF, and more.

- **Duration**: The Blogathon runs from September 29, 2023, to November 30, 2023.

- **Participation**: Participants should publish their articles on personal blogs or other public platforms and submit the links to OpenNTF.

**Rewards**

OpenNTF will evaluate submissions based on quality, innovation, and contribution to the community, offering rewards to outstanding entries.

**Guidelines**

- Submissions must be original and not previously published elsewhere.

- Content should be relevant to the HCL Domino ecosystem and provide practical insights or innovative ideas.

**Objective**

Through this Blogathon, OpenNTF aims to foster knowledge sharing, encourage innovative thinking, and enhance the vibrancy of the HCL Domino ecosystem.

**How to Participate**

Interested community members can visit the [OpenNTF official website](https://www.openntf.org/blogs/openntf.nsf/d6plinks/20230929-2023-OpenNTF-Blogathon) for more information and submission guidelines.

OpenNTF looks forward to the creative contributions from the community to drive the growth and development of the HCL Domino ecosystem.
