On June 17, 2025, HCLSoftware officially released HCL Domino 14.5, a collaboration platform specifically designed to enhance data privacy and security for governments and regulated organizations. ([hcltech.com](https://www.hcltech.com/press-releases/hclsoftware-launches-sovereign-ai-aimed-governments-and-regulated-organizations?utm_source=openai))

## Domino IQ: Built-in Sovereign AI Extension

A standout feature of Domino 14.5 is the introduction of Domino IQ, an AI extension integrated directly into the Domino platform. Domino IQ enables organizations to utilize models developed internally or by trusted sources, ensuring that data processing occurs entirely within the local environment. This approach eliminates reliance on external cloud services, aligning with regulations such as the European AI Act. ([hcl-software.com](https://www.hcl-software.com/blog/domino/hcl-domino-iq-trusted-ai-for-secure-sovereign-innovation?utm_source=openai))

## Enhanced Security and Compliance

Domino 14.5 has achieved information security certification from the German Federal Office for Information Security (BSI), further strengthening the platform's security credentials. Additionally, the release supports Security Event and Incident Management (SEIM) tools, aiding organizations in effectively monitoring and responding to security threats. ([hcltech.com](https://www.hcltech.com/press-releases/hclsoftware-launches-sovereign-ai-aimed-governments-and-regulated-organizations?utm_source=openai))

## European Accessibility Act Compliance

To improve user experience, Domino 14.5 complies with the European Accessibility Act, enhancing web-based business user interfaces to ensure broader accessibility. ([hcltech.com](https://www.hcltech.com/press-releases/hclsoftware-launches-sovereign-ai-aimed-governments-and-regulated-organizations?utm_source=openai))

## Deployment and Upgrade Information

HCL Domino 14.5 is now officially available. Existing customers and partners can download and deploy the release through the My HCL Software portal. HCLSoftware encourages all customers to upgrade to the latest version to take full advantage of new features and security enhancements. ([developer.ds.hcl-software.com](https://developer.ds.hcl-software.com/t/introducing-hcl-domino-14-5/171525?utm_source=openai))

In summary, the release of HCL Domino 14.5 marks a significant advancement in collaboration platforms, particularly for organizations prioritizing data sovereignty and compliance, by offering enhanced security, AI integration, and accessibility features.
