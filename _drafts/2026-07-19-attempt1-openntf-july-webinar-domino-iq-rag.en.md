OpenNTF is hosting a July webinar titled "Domino IQ RAG." Presented by Brian Arnold, the session will explore how the new RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) feature in Domino IQ can enhance AI responses within organizations. Brian will explain the workings of RAG, detail data requirements, address challenges associated with short data, and demonstrate several Domino applications utilizing the RAG feature.

The webinar is scheduled for July 2. For more information, visit the [OpenNTF official website](https://openntf.org/Internal/home.nsf/home.xsp).
